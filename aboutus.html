It is hard to stand out in the barbering business. Finding and maintaining loyal customers is your best way to make money. Keep the contact information of all of your customers and send them personalized messages. Start with emails, though you can make phone calls if you get very friendly with them. Make social media pages, especially on Facebook and Twitter. Follow local news channels and share stories that are important to your area and to barbering. Focus on providing great content that they cannot get elsewhere. You can write essays or articles about beauty trends you like.
